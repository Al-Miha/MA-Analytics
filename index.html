about

M·A Analytics CEE offers comprehensive one-stop-shop consulting services accros a broad spectrum of disciplines to Small and Medium Enterprizes that want to enter new CEE markets, through our hubs in Warsaw (Poland) for Central Europe, Sarajevo (Bosnia) and Belgrade (Serbia) for SEE region.

The core of our services are the solutions tailored for SMEs aiming to present their products and services or make purchases on CEE markets without having to conduct significant investments at the very start of market entry process. We put our local infrastructure at disposal to SMEs in all necessary segments, paired with our flexible service packages and pricing policies.

Starting from scratch, and without having any experience in the specific CEE market, with support of our experts, our clients have been able to get the required market intelligence data, hold meetings with their potential local clients or suppliers and conclude first trading transactions in a very short periods of time.
